Caffeine Solution 5% + EGCG

A depuffing solution for tired eyes.

About

Caffeine Solution 5% + EGCG is a water-based serum that reduces the look of puffiness and dark circles in the eye contour. The formula works by combining a high concentration of caffeine with a highly-purified epigallocatechin gallatyl glucoside (EGCG).

Use after cleansing, before water-based serums, anhydrous serums, oils, suspensions or creams.
